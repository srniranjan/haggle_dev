The Haggle platform allows users to leverage their social data and "haggle" for a personalised price.

To allow people to be able to do this, we:

Crawl

Have crawlers which fetch restaurant info from various social networks

Leverage

We allow users to leverage their social data on Facebook, Foursquare and Twitter

Harmonize

We harmonize and cache this information in our datastore to be able to quickly mine user preferences

Pull

Our nightly jobs pull all users' data from these networks harmonize them and mine for patterns in this data
