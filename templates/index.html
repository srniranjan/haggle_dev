Harmonize

Our proprietary harmonizing algorithms are built on top of both semantic web and NLP modules. Sound confusing? We piece together all of the crawled data across every platform with data. So, when you want to access Joe’s Deli at 29th and Park, Haggle’s API helps you know for sure that some apps call it Joe’s Sandwich Place and a site calls it Joe’s Cafe, then checkins and likes from different social networks, even all the slightly different locations, are all harmonized into one unique establishment.
